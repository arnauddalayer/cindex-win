Selecting records

Many Cindex operations are undertaken on entries that are selected (highlighted) in the main document window.

  • To select any entry click it.
  • To extend the selection drag (forward or backward) through the displayed entries, or press the SHIFT key while you click the entry that you want at the start or end of the range. You can also extend the selection by holding down the SHIFT key while you move through the index by hitting
    • UPARROW or DOWNARROW.
  • To select all the records under a particular main heading, move the mouse to the left margin of the window. The pointer will change from a left-arrow to a right-arrow, signifying that Cindex will select blocks of records. Move the mouse to the main heading of the block of records you want to select, then click. Cindex will select all the records under that heading. To select all the records under a particular subheading, place the mouse beside the subheading, then click. You can use the same method to select blocks of records at any lower level of heading.
  • To select all records that Cindex can display in the current view, choose Select All from the Edit menu.
  • To remove the highlighting from a selection, hit ESC.
  • To force selected records into view, hit TAB.
