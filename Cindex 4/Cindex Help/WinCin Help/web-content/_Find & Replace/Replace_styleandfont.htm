Replacing text styles and fonts

Use the Replace panel (Edit menu) to search for and replace text attributes.

To find records that contain (or do not contain) text formatted in a particular style and/or font, click Attributes… beside the Find box. Cindex will display a panel through which you can specify the attributes that the text must contain (or must not contain).

To change the attributes, click Attributes… beside the Change to box. Cindex will display a panel through which you can specify the attributes of the text you want to change.

  • To leave a style unchanged (either present or absent) click the appropriate button under Ignore.
  • To add a style click the appropriate button under Apply.
  • To remove a style click the appropriate button under Remove. (You can remove a style only if you specified it among Find attributes.)
  • To leave the font unchanged, click Ignore in the Font box.
  • To change the font click Change To in the Font box. Choose <Default> to restore the font to the default, or choose a font by name to apply a particular font.

To search for and replace styles or fonts regardless of the text they are attached to do the following:

  • Specify attributes to find, without specifying search text.
  • Specify replacement attributes without specifying replacement text.

To search among a subset of displayed records specify the Scope of the search.

To confine the search to records that have one or more special attributes check the relevant attributes in the box Only Among.
