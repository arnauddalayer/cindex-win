Using the Replace panel

Use the Replace panel (Edit menu) to search for and replace words and phrases, or text attributes.

Type in the Find box the word or phrase you want to replace; type in the Change to box the replacement text (leave this blank if you want to remove the text you are searching for).

To find the first occurrence of the text you want to replace, click Find; after Cindex has found the text, Click Replace to replace it.

To replace all occurrences of the text, click Replace All.

  • To replace text that is formatted in a particular style or font, click Attributes… beside the Find box. Cindex will display a panel through which you can specify the attributes of the text you are looking for.
  • To replace text styles and fonts, click Attributes… beside the Change to box. Cindex will display a panel through which you can specify the attributes you want to change.
  • To confine the search to specified levels of heading, choose from the Field popup menu.
  • To make Cindex accept only matches to whole words, check Whole Word.
  • To make Cindex accept only case-sensitive matches, check Case.
  • To make Cindex treat the search text as a pattern to be matched, construct the pattern in the Find box and check Pattern.

To search among a subset of displayed records specify the Scope of the search.

To confine the search to records that have one or more special attributes check the relevant attributes in the box Only Among.
