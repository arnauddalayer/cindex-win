Searching

Cindex provides several ways for you to search the index for particular records, words and phrases, fonts or styles.

When you are working in the main index window, you can move instantly to a particular record by typing leading text, or the record's number.

You can use Go To… (View menu) to bring into view a record whose heading and subheading begins with specified text, or to show the record that would lie at the top of a particular printed page.

You can use the Find panel (Edit menu) to find records contain specified text or text styles or fonts. You can also use the Find panel to find records that have particular attributes (e.g., carry a particular label) and to make conditional (Boolean) searches.
