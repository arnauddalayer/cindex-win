About customer support

We pride ourselves on the quality and efficiency of our technical support, which we provide free to registered users of Cindex. To ensure your eligibility for support, be sure to return your registration card to us.

We provide technical support via:

  • World Wide Web
  • Email
  • telephone

World Wide Web

Visit www.example.com/cindex for answers to a range of frequently-asked questions about Cindex. This information is revised regularly as new questions arise, and is a useful source of tips.

Email, Telephone

Before you contact us about a problem with Cindex, please check that you are using the most up-do-date version. From time to time we provide updates. These are designed to correct problems and improve performance. You can set Cindex to check automatically, and notify you, if an update is available. You can also check by choosing Check for Update from the Help menu.

When you contact us always include the following information:

  • Your registration number. To display your registration number, start Cindex and choose About�Cindex� from the Help menu.
  • The version number of your copy of Cindex. To display the version number, start Cindex and choose About�CINDEX� from the Help menu.
  • The version of the operating system you are using (Windows XP or Windows Vista).
  • If Cindex appears not to be working as it should, please provide a detailed description of the problem. Including information about the following points will make it easier for us to provide speedy and effective help:
    • Is the problem reproducible? Can you duplicate it by repeating the same set of steps? If the problem is reproducible, please tell us what steps lead to it.
    • If you can more easily describe your problem by providing us with a copy of your index, please include a copy as an Email attachment (or send it via regular mail).
